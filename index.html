Balance

At dawn's first light where shadows play,

Balance treads a thread so slight,

A dance of dark and gentle day,

Where harmony and discord fight.

The pendelum swings with measured grace,

In life's great scale where hopes and fears collide,

Each fleeting moment leaves its trace,

On the tightrope where our dreams reside.

Tobalanceis to find a steady path,

Amid the swirl, where calm and chaos bathe.
