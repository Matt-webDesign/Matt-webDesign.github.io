Must-Visit Destinations

Explore the Great Wall of China

The Great Wall of China

A marvel of ancient engineering, the Great Wall winds across mountains and valleys. Hike its trails for panoramic views and a walk through history.

The Forbidden City

Located in Beijing, this sprawling palace complex is a treasure trove of imperial culture. Wander through its courtyards and discover China's royal heritage.

The Terracotta Army

Unearthed in Xi'an, thousands of life-sized warriors and horses stand guard over Emperor Qin Shi Huang's tomb—a sight both haunting and magnificent.

Li River and Guilin

Cruise along the Li River to witness serene villages and surreal karst landscapes. Guilin's natural beauty has inspired poets and painters for centuries.
